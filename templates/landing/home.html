FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

StartupLens uses a structured pipeline: you submit your startup idea, and it goes through automated research, knowledge extraction, business intelligence analysis, assessment scoring, recommendation generation, roadmap planning, and final evaluation. Each step builds on the previous one to give you a comprehensive validation report.

No. All scores, grades, and readiness levels are calculated by deterministic business rules — not AI. AI is used only for commentary, SWOT analysis, and qualitative feedback. This ensures reproducible, trustworthy evaluations.

Yes. StartupLens generates professional PDF reports using ReportLab. Each report includes a SHA-256 integrity checksum, making it suitable for sharing with investors, mentors, and incubators.

Investor matching uses a compatibility scoring algorithm that compares your startup's industry, stage, and funding needs against investor preferences. Each match includes a detailed explanation of why the investor is a good fit.

StartupLens uses Random Forest and XGBoost classifiers trained on startup success patterns. The model registry tracks algorithm versions, accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 scores for full transparency.
